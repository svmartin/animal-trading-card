Chocolate Labrador

The chocolate lab is known for its love of children and desire to roll in fresh sh*#!

  • Scientific Name: Canus lupus familiris
  • Average weight: 25-32 kgs
  • Average lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: house or apartment

The chocolate lab is a great dog for families. In the winter months, the chocolate lab will keep your toes warm and in the summer months will keep you busy pulling trash from its mouth.
